Greyscalegorrilla

Greyscalegorilla is motion graphics/video plugins online store, where you can also
find free tutorials for many plugins for 2D and 3D programs. I really like the site
because it is very easy to navigate. The page layout is very simple, they have a logo,
important links on the top. The video, where you can watch tutorials occupies the center
of the page. Below the video there are other links that tell you about the plugin kit.
The colors are very easy on your eyes. If you decide to buy their product, the checkout
and downloads are on the same page. Most importantly there are no advertisements.
You can get the same products they sell on other sites, but the simple design is what
draws most motion designers to this site.

American Film Institute

American film institute's web site is one of the best film history sites. This site ranks
as one of the top film sites because it is easy to navigate and find any information on
any American film. The links are very clearly laid out on the top. Important events are
listed at the bottom. The background is a easy on the eyes grey, the color palette is grey
to white,which brings out the colorful film posters and pictures. The only annoying part
is the looping slide shows. Again, the layout is simple and not cluttered. There are three things that most want to find when they visit the site, the catalogs, AFI's Top 100 and
events,which you can find on the home page without having to search around.
