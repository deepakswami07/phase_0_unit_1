About Me

My name is Daniel Deepak. I am student at DBC New York City.
I live in Austin. I am a motion graphic artist, editor and teacher. I am
really excited to be returning to NYC to be part of DBC. I lived in NYC
for many years before I moved to Austin 8 years ago. I have really
missed the street food and the good public transportation. If you are
wondering what sort of motion graphics I do, you can take a look at my
showreel.I love films--both American and Indian, so I wanted to share
some of my favorites with you. If you have any questions you can
contact me by email.

  • Favorite Hollywood Epics

Some of my favorite Hollywood films have been epics. I was always

Here's my favorite five:

  1. Ben-Hur
  2. Quo-Vadis
  3. Lawence of Arabia
  4. The Ten Commandments
  5. Omar Mukhtar

  • Favorite Bollywood Films

Bollywood is one subsection of Indian films, which are dividied by
languages. Every region has its own film industry in its own language.
Bollywood refers Indian films made in Hindi in Mumbai (formerly known
as Bombay). Many of these are the ones that are famous for their cheesy
over-the-top melodramas. They are a lot of fun to watch!
Here's my favorite five

  1. Sholay
  2. Ganga-Jamuna
  3. Mughal-E-Azam
  4. Pyaasa
  5. Guide

  • Favorite "Film Noir"

I love black and white films, and what stands out about this genre
is the character development which is really complex, the lighting
and everything grey about it.
Here's my favorite five:

  1. Double Indemnity
  2. Gaslight
  3. Out of the Past
  4. China Town
  5. The Third Man
