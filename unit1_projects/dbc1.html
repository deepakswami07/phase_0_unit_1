About Me

    My name is Daniel Deepak. I am student at DBC New York City. I live in Austin. I am a motion graphic artist, editor and teacher. I am really excited to be returning to NYC to be part of DBC. I lived in NYC for many years before I moved to Austin 8 years ago. I have really missed the street food and the good public transportation. If you are wondering what sort of motion graphics I do, you can take a look at my showreel. I love films--both American and Indian, so I wanted to share some of my favorites with you. If you have any questions you can contact me by email.

    • Favorite Hollywood Epics

      • Some of my favorite Hollywood films have been epics. I was always fascinated
      by watching epics on 70 mm screens wide high resolution films which are able to
      capture the spectacle of these films. Here's my favorite five:

      1. Ben-Hur
      2. Quo-Vadis
      3. Lawence of Arabia
      4. The Ten Commandments
      5. Omar Mukhtar

    • Favorite Bollywood Films

      • Bollywood is one subsection of Indian films, which are dividied by languages.
      Every region has its own film industry in its own language. Bollywood refers
      Indian films made in Hindi in Mumbai (formerly known as Bombay). Many of these
      are the ones that are famous for their cheesy over-the-top melodramas. They are
      a lot of fun to watch!
      Here's my favorite five

      1. Sholay
      2. Ganga-Jamuna
      3. Mughal-E-Azam
      4. Pyaasa
      5. Guide

    • Favorite "Film Noir"

      • I love black and white films, and what stands out about this genre is the character
      development which is really complex, the lighting and everything grey about it.
      Here's my favorite five:

      1. Double Indemnity
      2. Gaslight
      3. Out of the Past
      4. China Town
      5. The Third Man
    • Favorite Westerns

      • I also love Westerns. When I was growing up in India, Westerns represented everything
      you needed to know about American culture. We thought everyone was a cowboy.
      Here's my favorite five:

      1. Once upon a time in the West
      2. High Noon
      3. The Searches
      4. TThe Magnificient Seven
      5. The Good, the Bad and the Ugly
    • Favorite Horror Films

      • This genre used to be my favorite but I don't watch them anymore because they give
      me nightmares. Here's my favorite five:

      1. Rosemary's Baby
      2. The Exorcist
      3. Alien
      4. The Evil Dead
      5. The Shinning
